About Me

Hi there! My name is Gabrielle Lowe. I was born in Ottawa, Ontario on August 5th 1999, making my zodiac a Leo. I firstly became interested in media design, specifically in photography and videography during my years in high school however, quickly lost touch of this love as I was heavily involved in other arts. From the early age of five, I quickly discovered I had a passion for the art of dance after being enrolled in my first ballet class. After dancing for nearly thirteen years, I decided to follow my dreams of pursuing a dance career and got accepted into the Ryerson University School of Performance dance program in 2017.

Now, being a recent graduate of the program, obtaining my BFA in 2021, I was unsure of where my career was going to take me next, especially during a global pandemic. During those times, I found myself reconnecting with my old passion for videography and ended up creating multiple short dance films of my own for various virtual dance festivals.

Eventually, I decided that I wanted to further my knowledge of videography and wanted to take it more seriously, hence my decision for enrolling into this program. I decided that Interactive Media Design would be the right program for me as I could take this passion for creating films and possibly turn it into a sustainable career path post-graduation. Ultimately, finding a career that could combine both my passion for dance and videography would be a dream.
